Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor,
Pala, Kerala is one among the IIITs that have been established 
as Institutions of National Importance by Ministry of Education, Govt. of India under the ambit of IIIT (PPP) Act 2017.
IIIT Kottayam campus is situated at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam - 
a picturous location, spread over in 53 acres of land -
allotted by Govt. of Kerala in the southern part of the state commonly referred to as Gods Own Country.
